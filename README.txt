This repository holds data for a paper accepted at PNAS, 2023 entitled "Ribosome-binding antibiotics increase bacterial longevity and growth efficiency" whose corresponding authors are Emily Wood (E.J.Wood@exeter.ac.uk) and Robert Beardmore (r.e.beardmore@exeter.ac.uk).

The data is held in XLSX and CSV and formats. These files mirror the same data whereby the correspondance between the CSV and XLSX files follows the convention that each spreadsheet sheet corresponds to one CSV file.

The CSV filenames and the spreadsheet sheet names correspond to figures from the stated paper and the descriptions of these figures in the paper should provide enough information to interpret the meaning of the data. If you find this is not the case, please email the authors at the above email addresses without hesitation.

Emily and Robert
